================
== HOW TO USE ==
================
Copy and paste this folder into your Unreal Engine project files:
Unreal Projects > [Your Project] > Content > Characters

That's it! When you open your project, you'll find all the Textures, Materials, Animations, and Models ready to use in the "Characters" folder.

===============
== COMO USAR ==
===============
Copia y pega esta capeta en los archivos de tu proyecto de Unreal Engine.
Unreal Projects > [Tu Proyecto] > Content > Characters
Y listo, al momento de abrir tu proyecto en la carpeta "Characters" encontraras todas las Texturas, Materiales, Animaciones y Modelos listos para ser usados.
